# Vision

## What PetStory is

Intelligent pet health app. Logs the animal's daily life (meals, symptoms, behavior, events) and uses AI to correlate patterns and generate clinical insights.

## Differentiator

Connects micro-details of daily life with medical history, creating a "prontuário vivo" (living medical record) that no current app does. The chat is both the input channel and the complexity layer; the diary is the distilled narrative.

## MVP scope

Focused on the tutor side:

- Intelligent conversational diary (chat → structured events auto-extracted)
- Event timeline (auto-generated from chat)
- Pattern correlation and proactive nudges
- Vet export (PDF / shareable link)

## Business model

Freemium. Free tier: daily logging, diary, basic reminders. Premium: contextual AI (deep correlations, long-horizon reasoning), longitudinal reports, multi-pet, shared access, data export.

## Domain & brand

- Domain: **petstory.co**
- Casing: lowercase **petstory** (matches domain, modern tech convention)
- Brand identity: see [brand.md](brand.md)

## Audience

Everyday pet owners, not data-literate users. Design priorities derive from this:

- Conversational > form-based
- Text-first, visualizations secondary
- Warm, personal tone (never clinical)
- No charts that require axis interpretation
